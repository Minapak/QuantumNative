1. Introduction

Welcome to QuantumNative ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our QuantumNative mobile application and related services.

Our Commitment: We believe in transparency and giving you control over your personal data. We only collect information necessary to provide you with the best quantum computing learning experience.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address, username, and password when you create an account
  • Profile Information: Learning preferences, skill level, and interests you choose to share
  • Payment Information: Processed securely through Apple's App Store; we do not store credit card details
  • Communications: Messages you send us for support or feedback

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: Learning progress, completed lessons, quiz scores, and time spent in the app
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and app version
  • Analytics: App performance data and crash reports to improve our service

2.3 Quantum Computing Data

  • Circuit Data: Quantum circuits you create and execute
  • Execution Results: Results from quantum simulations and hardware executions
  • Portfolio Evidence: Certificates and achievements you earn

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide Services: Deliver personalized quantum computing education and track your progress
  • Improve Experience: Analyze usage patterns to enhance our curriculum and features
  • Process Payments: Manage subscriptions and premium features
  • Communications: Send learning reminders, updates, and respond to inquiries
  • Security: Protect against fraud and ensure platform integrity
  • Legal Compliance: Meet regulatory requirements and respond to legal requests

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Service Providers: Cloud hosting (AWS), analytics, and payment processing partners
  • Quantum Hardware Partners: IBM Quantum for circuit execution (anonymized data only)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Business Transfers: In connection with mergers or acquisitions

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure cloud infrastructure (AWS with encryption at rest)
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Access controls and authentication mechanisms
  • Secure token-based authentication (JWT)

6. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Portability: Export your learning progress and achievements
  • Opt-out: Disable notifications and marketing communications
  • Restriction: Limit certain data processing activities

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@swiftquantum.tech

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Upon account deletion:

  • Personal data is deleted within 30 days
  • Anonymized analytics data may be retained for research
  • Legal/compliance records retained as required by law

8. Children's Privacy

QuantumNative is designed for users of all ages interested in quantum computing education. For users under 13 (or applicable age in your jurisdiction):

  • We require parental consent for account creation
  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without consent
  • Parents can request deletion of their child's data

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in:

  • South Korea: Primary data center (AWS ap-northeast-2)
  • United States: IBM Quantum services

We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers, including standard contractual clauses and compliance with applicable data protection laws.

10. Region-Specific Rights

European Economic Area (GDPR)

If you are in the EEA, you have additional rights under GDPR including the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

California (CCPA)

California residents have the right to know what personal information is collected, request deletion, and opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell data).

South Korea (PIPA)

Korean users have rights under the Personal Information Protection Act, including access, correction, and deletion rights.

Japan (APPI)

Japanese users have rights under the Act on Protection of Personal Information.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through:

  • In-app notifications
  • Email (if you have an account)
  • Updated "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
